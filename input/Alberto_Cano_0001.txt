About

Alberto Cano is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) in Richmond, Virginia. He leads the High-Performance Data Mining research lab. His research is focused on machine learning, data streams, imbalanced data, multi-label learning, evolutionary computation, and their scalability on GPUs. He is the Faculty Director of the VCU's High Performance Research Computing Core Facility: https://hprc.vcu.edu/
