Antonios Anastasopoulos
Assistant Professor
George Mason University, ENGR 4412
Collaborating Senior Researcher, Archimedes AI Research Center

Antonis Anastasopoulos is an assistant professor at George Mason Computer Science. He is also a Collaborating Senior Researcher at the Archimedes AI Research Center in Athens, Greece. He is interested in various aspects of multilingual Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, with a main focus on Machine Translation and Speech Recognition for endangered languages and low-resource settings in general.
