Welcome to the Uhler Lab.
The Uhler Lab develops machine learning foundations and methods for integrating different data modalities and inferring causal relationships from such data. The developed methods and algorithms are applied to discover the regulatory circuits underlying the programs of cells and tissues in health and disease.
