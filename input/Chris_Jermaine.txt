I am the J.S. Abercrombie Professor of Engineering, a Professor of Computer Science, and the chair of the Department of Computer Science at Rice University. Along with a wonderful group of PhD students, I design and build systems for large-scale, computationally intensive data processing, with a focus on systems for machine learning and AI. I teach classes in database system design and implementaiton, and in data science.
