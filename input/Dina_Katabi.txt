Research
Digital Health

New digital biomarkers and contactless health monitoring of patients with COVID, Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, etc.

Wireless and Mobile System

New sensors, algorithms, and systems that see through wall, improve data communications, and build the Internet of Things

Machine Learning & Computer Vision

Capturing the human pose, shape, and actions through walls and occlusions using custom neural models that operate on RF signals.
