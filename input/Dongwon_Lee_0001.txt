Description: Welcome to the home of the PIKE (Penn State Information, Knowledge, and wEb) Research Group. We study research issues involving the management of and mining in data in diverse forms (e.g., structured records, text, multimedia, social media, and Web), and their social or security applications. People seem to call such as (part of) Data Science, Social Computing, and/or Cybersecurity these days.
