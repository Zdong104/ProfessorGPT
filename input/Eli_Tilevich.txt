Research interests: Systems end of Software Engineering; distributed systems and middleware; automated software transformation; mobile applications; energy efficient software; security and privacy; CS education; music informatics

Research
My research interests lie on the Systems end of Software Engineering, with a particular emphasis on distributed systems, mobile/IoT applications, middleware, software energy efficiency, software security, automated program transformation, music informatics, and CS education. I have published over 130 refereed research papers on these subjects. My research awards include a Microsoft Research Software Engineering Innovation Foundation Award and an IBM Faculty Award. At Virginia Tech, I lead the Software Innovations Lab. The lab's research projects have been supported by major US federal funding agencies and private industry.
