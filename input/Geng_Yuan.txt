Geng Yuan

Geng Yuan is an assistant professor in School of Computing (CS Department) at the University of Georgia. He received his Ph.D. Degree in Computer Engineering from Northeastern University (NEU) in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Yanzhi Wang.

 

His research interests are in general AI systems, including energy-efficient deep learning and artificial intelligence systems, efficient training, model compression, hardware-software co-design for DNN architectures, and emerging deep learning systems.
