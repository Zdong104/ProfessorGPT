Biography

Hanumant Singh is a professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a courtesy appointment in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences. He is a senior member of IEEE and a member of the American Geophysical Union. Additionally, he is a member of Eta Kappa Nu and Alpha Chi honor societies.
