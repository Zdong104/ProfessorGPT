About Huy Lê
Biography

Huy Lê Nguyen is an associate professor of computer science in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University. Prior to joining Northeastern, he was a research assistant professor at the Toyota Technological Institute in Chicago and a Google Research Fellow at the Simons Institute at University of California, Berkeley. Nguyen is broadly interested in the design and analysis of algorithms, with an emphasis on algorithmic techniques for massive data sets and machine learning.
