Biography

Dr. Jennifer G. Dy is an associate professor at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a courtesy Khoury College appointment. She obtained her master’s and doctorate degrees in 1997 and 2001 respectively from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University and her bachelor’s in 1993 from the Department of Electrical Engineering at University of the Philippines.

Dy received an NSF Career award in 2004. She has been an editorial board member for the journal, Machine Learning since 2004 and program committee member for ICML, ACM SIGKDD, AAAI, and SIAM SDM. Additionally, she was publications chair for the International Conference on Machine Learning in 2004.
