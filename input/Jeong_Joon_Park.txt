I'm a new assisant professor at the University of Michigan CSE (Office: BBB2717).
I'm broadly interested in computer vision, graphics, and artificial intelligence. My current research focus is on realistic 3D content generation using physical and neural representations: generations of large-scale, dynamic, and interactive 3D scenes are primary targets. My group will explore 3D vision, graphics and machine learning, and their applications to robotics, medical imaging, and scientific problems.

I'm actively looking for students and postdoc applicants to join my group at UMichigan!
Please refer to the note below for details. I encourage interested students to apply to the UMichigan CSE PhD program and mention my name in the application. If you have a strong interest, please feel free to drop me an email.
