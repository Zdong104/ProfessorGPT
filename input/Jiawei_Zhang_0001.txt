About Me (CV)

I'm an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at University of California, Davis. I founded IFM Lab in 2017, and have been working as the director since then. IFM Lab is a research oriented academic laboratory, providing the latest academic research works and application tools to both academia and industry.
