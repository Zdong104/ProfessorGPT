Members: Joohyung Lee, Adam Ishay

We are a research group interested in knowledge representation and reasoning, logic programming, commonsense reasoning, the computational semantics of natural language, logics in security, and cognitive robotics.
We are part of the Artificial Intelligence (AI) group, which is part of the School of Computing, Informatics and Decision Systems Engineering, under Fulton Schools of Engineering at Arizona State University.
We are one of the TAG teams.
