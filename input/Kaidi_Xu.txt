I’m looking for highly motivated students who have strong background in machine learning and deep learning, with full financial support. Interested candidates are strongly encouraged to contact me via email, together with a resume including publication list and transcripts.

Kaidi Xu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Drexel University. He obtained his Ph.D. degree at Northeastern University in 2021 advised by Prof. Xue (Shelley) Lin. His primary research interest is trustworthy AI, including formal neural network verification, real-world adversarial attacks, and certified defenses with applications in healthcare, material science, and other interdisciplinary science. His research papers have been published in various top conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, IJCAI, AAAI, CVPR, ECCV, ICCV, etc. Previously to Northeastern University, He obtained M.S. and B.S. degrees from the Department of Computer Science at University of Florida in 2017 and Sichuan University in 2015 respectively.
