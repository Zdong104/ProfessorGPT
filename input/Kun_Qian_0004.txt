About

Kun Qian is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. He is also a member of the Link Lab. Before joining UVA, he was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California San Diego. He received his Ph.D. and B.E. from Tsinghua University in 2019 and 2014. His research interests center around ubiquitous intelligence enabled by wireless sensing technologies, with their applications in existing and emerging physical-aware scenarios, such as next-generation communication, the Internet of Things, autonomous driving, and mobile computing.
