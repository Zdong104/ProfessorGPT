About me

I am assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. degree from Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Deliang Fan, Master’s degree at University of Central Florida, and Bachelor’s degree at Northeastern University, China.

My research interests include:
