Michael(Misha)Chertkov

I was educated in Russia (M.Sc. '90, Novosibirsk State U) and Israel (Ph.D. '96, Weizmann Institute), spent '96-'99 as a Dicke fellow at Princeton U (1996-99) and moved to Los Alamos, NM in 1999, first as an Oppenheimer fellow and then as a staff member in  Theory Division and Center for Nonlinear Studies at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL).

Relocated to Tucson in 2019, where I am the  Chair of Program in Applied Mathematics at the University of Arizona.
