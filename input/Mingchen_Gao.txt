Short Bio:

Currently I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University at Buffalo.

I spent three years as a Postdoctoral Fellow in Department of Radiology and Imaging Science of the Clinical Center at National Institutes of Health.

I received PhD in Computer Science from Rutgers University in 2014, under supervision of Prof. Dimitris N. Metaxas. I received my B.S. degree from Chien-shiung Wu College (Gifted Young Class), Southeast University, Nanjing, China in 2007.
