INTERESTS
Robotics, artificial intelligence, dynamical systems, and applied mathematics.
BIOGRAPHY

Nilanjan is the director of the Interacting Robotic Systems Laboratory. Previously, he was a Project Scientist and Postdoc at The Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. He obtained his PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

RESEARCH

Nilanjan's research interests are in studying and understanding the algorithmic foundations of interacting intelligent systems (e.g., robotic systems, cyber-physical systems, intelligent software agents).
