Coaching Purdue ICPC Team
On May 29, 2023, Purdue Team (Trung Dang, Richard Li, and Devin Qu) received a Bronze Medal in ICPC North America Championship. Purdue team finished at 4th place.
On February 25, 2023, Purdue Team (Trung Dang, Richard Li, and Devin Qu) won ICPC East Central North America Contest, besting teams from CMU, Waterloo, Toronto, Michigan and many other universities in the region.
On November 10, 2022, Purdue Team (Riley Borgard, Trung Dang, and Richard Li) finished 20th place in ICPC World Finals Dhaka. Purdue was 4rd place among all universities in North America behind MIT, CMU, and Waterloo.
On May 30, 2022, Purdue Team (Trung Dang, Minh Nguyen, and Devin Qu) finished 9th place in ICPC North America Championship, and advanced to World Final .
On August 14, 2021, Purdue Team (Riley Borgard, Trung Dang, and Richard Li) finished 12th place in ICPC North America Championship, and advanced to World Final Dhaka.
On February 23, 2020, Purdue Team (Riley Borgard, Trung Dang, and Yi Xu) won Bronze Medal (4th place) in 2020 North America Championship, and advanced to World Final Moscow. Due to Covid, only Riley attended WF Moscow.
