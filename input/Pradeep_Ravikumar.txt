Description: Pradeep Ravikumar

Professor
Machine Learning Department, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University




	

I lead the Statistical & Symbolic Learning (Neuro-Symbolic AI) Group in the Machine Learning Department, School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University.

I am co-Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR), the premier journal for the field of machine learning. I was previously Associate Editor-in-Chief for IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (TPAMI).

I have received the Sloan Research Fellowship, the National Science Foundation's CAREER Award, and the Siebel Scholarship. My thesis has won honorable mentions in the ACM SIGKDD Dissertation Award, and the CMU School of Computer Science Distinguished Dissertation Award.
