Qi Yu, PhD

Professor 
Graduate Program Director, School of Information
Director, Machine Learning and Data Intensive Computing Lab

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, M.E from National University of Singapore, Singapore, and B.S from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China. For more details check my CV.

Research interests: 
Machine Learning and Deep Learning
Active Learning, few-shot learning, and meta-learning
Uncertainty in Machine Learning and Deep Learning
Weakly supervised learning, multiple instance learning
Dynamic Data Modeling
Multimodal Data Fusion
Bayesian Nonparametrics
