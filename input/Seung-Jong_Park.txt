Research Interests

Dr. Park has performed interdisciplinary research in the following areas:

Distributed computing ranging from cloud computing over high speed optical networks to mobile computing over wireless networks
Big data research including developing software frameworks for large-scale genome sequence analysis, large-scale molecular dynamic simulation over heterogeneous computing environment including cloud computing and high performance computing
Cyberinfrastructure development for large-scale scientific and engineering applications
