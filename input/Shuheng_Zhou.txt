Shuheng Zhou's homepage

Welcome! I am an Associate Professor of Statistics at University of California, Riverside. My research spans high dimensional statistics, algorithms, privacy and data science.

I will be on Sabbatical leave from July 2024 to Sept. 2025, except for the Winter quarter of 2025, during which I will be on campus and teach two courses.
