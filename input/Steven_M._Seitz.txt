Steve Seitz
Robert E. Dinning Professor
Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering
University of Washington
seitz@cs.washington.edu

I'm a professor at UW, where I do research on computer vision, computer graphics, and related topics.  I co-direct the UW Reality Lab and am affiliated with GRAIL.

I'm also a Distinguished Scientist at Google on the Project Starline team, and previously worked on VR, Photos, Computational Photography, and Maps.
