Tony R. Martinez

Tony Martinez is a professor of Computer Science at Brigham Young University in the Computer Science Department. He directs the Neural Network and Machine Learning Laboratory in the BYU PhD/MS program. His main research interests are in machine learning, neural networks, massively parallel algorithms and architectures, and non von Neumann computing methods. He is a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.
