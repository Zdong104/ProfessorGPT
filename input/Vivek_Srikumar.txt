I am an associate professor in the Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah. I co-lead the Utah NLP group here and am part of the Utah Center for Data Science.

My research deals primarily with Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

Currently teaching

CS 5350/6350, DS 4350 — Machine Learning, Spring 2024

Here’s a catalog of class lectures on various topics on machine learning and NLP.

Research Patrons

My research has been supported by the US National Science Foundation (NSF), the US-Israel Binational Science Foundation (BSF) and the National Institute for Health (NIH). In addition, the following companies have generously supported by work either via awards and compute gifts: Intel, Google, Verisk, Bloomberg, and Nvidia.

Previous affiliations

I was a visiting researcher at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence during my 2022 sabbatical year.

I was a post-doctoral researcher at Stanford University at the Stanford NLP group during 2013-14.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2013.
