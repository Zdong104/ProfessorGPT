I am an Associate Professor in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at the Pennsylvania State University. My research bridges the areas of big data, data mining, machine learning, database and biomedical informatics. I am particularly interested in developing algorithms and models for analyzing large data sets generated in social, biological and medical domains.
