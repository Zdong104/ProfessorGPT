Short Bio

Dr. Tong received her Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, New York. She is currently a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Carolina. Before joining USC, she has been working in Visualization and Computer Vision Lab of GE Global Research Center since January 2008.




Research Interests

Dr. Tong’s general research interests are on computer vision, machine learning, and human computer interaction, especially in

·       Affective computing (facial activity analysis and eye gaze tracking)

·       Probabilistic graphical models and their applications in computer vision including modeling, reasoning, information fusion, and learning under uncertainty

·       Machine learning and its applications in computer vision and interdisciplinary research such as psychology, cybersecurity, HCI, marketing, materials science, mechanical engineering, and mobile computing
