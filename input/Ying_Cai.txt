Position
Associate Professor
Ying Cai received his Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Central Florida in 2002. He joined the Department of Computer Science at Iowa State University in 2003 and is now an associate professor there. His research interests include cloud computing, privacy and security, mobile computing, and multimedia systems.
